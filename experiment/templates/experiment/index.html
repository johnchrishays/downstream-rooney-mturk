Research Study Summary:

There are no known or anticipated risks to you other than a possible loss of confidentiality. You will be compensated with a base payment of ${{ base_reward }} and an average bonus payment of ${{ avg_bonus_reward }}.

How will you keep my data safe and private?

Your data would be stored electronically. Participation is anonymous. We will not know your name. We will not be able to connect any identifying information to your survey answers. However, we will know your MTurk number in order to pay you for your time. Your MTurk number could possibly be connected to your public profile, which could, in theory, be searched.
